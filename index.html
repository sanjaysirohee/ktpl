Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our laptop and computer rental services in Delhi NCR. Still have questions? Contact us directly.

1. What is the minimum rental period?

We offer flexible rental periods starting from 1 day to long-term contracts. Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly rental options are available.

2. Do you provide bulk rental discounts?

Yes, we offer attractive discounts for bulk orders (10+ units). Corporate clients get special pricing for long-term rentals and volume orders.

3. Is rental available for short-term events?

Absolutely! We specialize in short-term rentals for events, conferences, training programs, and temporary office setups across Delhi NCR.

4. What areas do you serve in Delhi NCR?

We serve all major areas including Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, Ghaziabad, and Faridabad with free delivery and pickup services.

5. Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide complete technical support including setup, configuration, troubleshooting, and on-site assistance for all rental equipment.

6. What types of laptops are available?

We have a wide range including MacBook Pro/Air, Dell, HP, Lenovo with Core i3/i5/i7 processors, 8GB+ RAM, and SSD storage options.

7. Is advance booking required?

For better availability, we recommend advance booking, especially for bulk orders and specific configurations. However, we try to accommodate urgent requirements.

8. What is included in the rental?

All rentals include the device, charger, carrying case (for laptops), basic software, and technical support. Custom software installation available on request.
